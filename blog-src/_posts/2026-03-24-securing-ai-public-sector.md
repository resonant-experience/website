After spending the last few years in public sector cyber consulting, we've been thinking about whether Australia's cyber security foundations are ready for AI. We decided to research this thoroughly. Cyber security is the critical enabler for the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of government systems and the data of Australians who depend on them. Trust in government AI depends on trust in the security foundations underneath it.

The result is: [Securing AI in Australia's Public Sector: Cyber Security Readiness for the AI Era]({{ '/assets/downloads/Securing_AI_Public_Sector_Final.pdf' | relative_url }})

* **142** primary sources
* **17** recommendations across policy, frameworks, technology, and workforce
* Each tagged Now, Next, or Later — with dependency logic and ownership mapped
